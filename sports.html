Football

Real Madrid

The Team:

The Spanish professional football club, Real Madrid is one of the most successful teams in the world. It was founded in 1902. The team's colors are white and navy blue, and their home ground is the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium in Madrid. The team's resilience and fighting spirit is and has always been generationally unmatched.

The Legends:

Real Madrid has been home to some of the greatest football players of all time, including Cristiano Ronaldo, Alfredo Di Stéfano, Ferenc Puskás, Zinedine Zidane, and Raúl González. Each of these players has made significant contributions to the team's success and helped cement Real Madrid's place as one of the most dominant teams in football history.
